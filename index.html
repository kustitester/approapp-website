Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan NFC tags?

Simply open the app and hold your phone near an NFC tag at participating venues.

How do I collect stamps?

Visit bars and scan their NFC tags to collect digital stamps and unlock rewards.

How do I find nearby bars?

Use the interactive map in the app to discover bars and events in your area.
